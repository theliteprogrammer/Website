About Us

At Recygo, we are passionate about making a positive impact on the environment and the community we serve. We are your trusted partner in Johannesburg, dedicated to providing comprehensive recycling waste removal services that align with your specific needs and preferences. Our mission is simple: "To be proud of our working environment."

Services We Provide

At Recygo, we provide a wide range of services tailored to meet your recycling needs, all within the vibrant city of Johannesburg. Our services include the collection and responsible disposal of various recyclable materials, ensuring your commitment to a cleaner environment is effortless. .

What We Collect

Paper & Cardboard: Don't let your old newspapers and cardboard boxes go to waste. We collect paper and cardboard materials, giving them a new life through recycling.

Aluminium: From beverage cans to aluminum foil, we collect all forms of aluminum waste, helping to conserve valuable resources and reduce energy consumption.

E-Waste: Dispose of your electronic waste responsibly with our e-waste collection service. We ensure that outdated electronics are properly recycled to minimize environmental impact.

What We Collect

Plastic: We collect various types of plastic materials, contributing to the reduction of plastic pollution and supporting the circular economy.

Glass: Say goodbye to glass waste piling up in your home. Our glass collection service ensures that glass containers are recycled efficiently.

Scrap Metal: If you have scrap metal lying around, we'll collect it and deliver it to recycling facilities, promoting sustainability in the metal industry.
